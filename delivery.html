Pargo Click‑&‑Collect Delivery

AfterGlow Gifts will prepare your order and hand it over to Pargo. Pargo then delivers it to your chosen collection point, making pickup easy and convenient for you.

Flat-rate collection fee: R90 per order

How It Works

  1. Select your preferred Pargo point from the dropdown.
  2. AfterGlow hands the parcel to Pargo.
  3. Pargo delivers it to your selected collection point.
  4. You’ll receive an SMS and email with a unique code when it arrives.
  5. Collect your parcel within 8 days using the code and your ID.

Benefits of Pargo

  • Choose from over 4,000 pickup points nationwide.
  • Quick delivery—typically within 1–2 days.
  • SMS and email notifications for your parcel.
  • Convenient pickup at your leisure.
  • Secure and discreet service—no AfterGlow branding where it matters most.

For assistance, track your parcel using Pargo’s Track & Trace or contact us.

Delivery & Courier

All orders are delivered door-to-door by Pingo or Courier Guy. No free delivery option is available.

Courier options:

  • Pingo: a fast, reliable on‑demand delivery partner, offering 1‑hour or scheduled deliveries :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  • Courier Guy: national courier network with door‑to‑door, locker and kiosk options :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Please note that:

  • Deliveries do not occur on weekends or public holidays.
  • Pingo and Courier Guy collect parcels from our warehouse same day, and you’ll receive your waybill by email.
  • All parcels are packaged discreetly—plain brown boxes with unbranded waybills and no reference to AfterGlow Gifts.
  • Standard delivery takes 3–5 working days; remote areas may take 1–2 extra days.
  • Orders placed over weekends or on public holidays will be processed the next working day.
  • Orders must be delivered within 24 hours of collection or reported. Please email info@afterglowgifts.co.za. Call us on 087 809 2500 if you don’t receive a response within 24 hours.

Delivery Rates

Flat-rate courier fee: R90 per order (regardless of order value).
